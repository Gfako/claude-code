---
name: Link outreach campaign rules
description: Key rules and preferences for running link building outreach campaigns — discovery approach, classification rules, outreach tracks
type: feedback
---

Search by use cases, not tool names. Don't search for "video translator" — search for articles discussing the pain points a video translator solves (multilingual training, global teams, content localization, etc.).

**Why:** Searching for the tool name finds competitor pages and direct alternatives. Searching for use cases finds articles where a link to our tool adds genuine value to readers.

**How to apply:** For every target page, brainstorm 8-12 use cases/pain points it solves, then build search queries around those.

---

Don't filter pages by URL pattern or word count to determine if they're articles. Our discovery already targets blog content via Google search, so nearly everything is editorial.

**Why:** URL patterns are unreliable ("post", "article", or nothing in path). Short pages are usually scrape failures, not non-articles.

**How to apply:** Skip any URL-pattern or word-count-based article detection. Trust the discovery queries.

---

Pages mentioning Synthesia should be excluded from outreach entirely.

**Why:** User doesn't want to reach out to pages that already mention the brand — they already have awareness.

---

Pages mentioning competitors are opportunities, not exclusions. Reclassify as strong fit.

**Why:** If a page links to HeyGen/Veed/etc, there's a concrete pitch — replace or add Synthesia alongside.

---

Two outreach tracks from the same prospect pool:
- Strong + medium fit → niche edit (personalized, reference specific section)
- Weak + no fit → generic pitch (topic-adjacent, pitch value for their audience)

**Why:** Even "no fit" pages were discovered through use-case searches, so their audience is relevant. The pitch angle just needs to be broader.

**How to apply:** All prospects get outreached — the email template changes based on fit strength, not whether they get contacted at all.
