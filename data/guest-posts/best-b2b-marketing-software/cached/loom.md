What's the difference between buying Loom on Loom.com versus Atlassian.com?

Loom was acquired by [Atlassian](https://www.loom.com/blog/loom-atlassian) last year, and we are in the process of integrating systems. As of October 23, 2024, there are some differences in feature access if you sign up for Loom through [Atlassian.com](https://www.atlassian.com/software/loom/pricing) vs. Loom.com. We'll take you through key differences so you can make an informed purchase decision. Please note that these differences exist for a limited time while we integrate Loom and Atlassian platforms more deeply, and we'll update this article regularly.

For a limited time, only purchases on [Atlassian.com](https://www.atlassian.com/software/loom/pricing) will have access to:

- Atlassian’s Administration hub, which allows you to administer your organization, apps, and users, and billing for all Atlassian apps.
- Loom recording entry points from within Confluence and Jira menus and sidebar placements.
- The same SSO/SCIM provisioning as other Atlassian apps, through Atlassian Guard.
