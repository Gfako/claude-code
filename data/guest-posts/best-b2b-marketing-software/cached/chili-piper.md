## **Common questions**

When does the new pricing go live?

Our new pricing tiers are rolling out now. If you're an existing customer, your account team will reach out with details on what changes for your account and when.

Is monthly billing available?

All Fire plans are billed annually. Annual billing allows us to offer volume discounts and multi-year savings. Monthly billing is not available at this time.

How do AI credits work?

AI credits are consumed when AI agents perform actions — identifying visitors, routing leads, running chat conversations, and more. Each plan includes a set number of credits per year. If you need more, you can purchase additional credits in bundles at any time.

Do volume discounts stack with multi-year discounts?

Yes. Volume discounts and multi-year discounts both apply automatically and are stackable. The calculator above shows your blended price in real time.

When will Chili Data Platform (Tier 3) be available?

The Chili Data Platform is coming soon. If you're already thinking about it, talk to us now — we're scoping early enterprise deals and can work with you on custom terms while we finalize the general availability pricing.
