## How does Demandbase pricing work?

What is available for purchase?

Demandbase One is our flagship account-based GTM platform for Sales and Marketing. We also offer our Advertising solution as a starting place, and our Data solutions can be used on their own to integrate our B2B data and AI-driven insights into your existing systems.

How does the pricing work for Demandbase One™ for Sales & Marketing?

Demandbase One for Sales and Marketing is designed for flexibility and simplicity. Our pricing model features a clear platform fee that covers all the essential software and services. In addition, there’s a flat fee per user, allowing you to easily scale according to your needs.

Can I buy standalone solutions for Marketing or Sales?

Our platform is built to change the way B2B organizations go to market by bringing Sales and Marketing together under one roof, using unified data, tools, and insights. That’s why we don’t split Demandbase One into separate solutions. Instead, we’ll work with you to tailor a plan that fits all of your GTM teams and their unique needs.
