## FAQS

##### You bring the questions, we’ll bring the answers

## Can I pay monthly?

Yes! Although we contract on an annual term, monthly billing is available if that works better for you (and your finance team).

## Do you offer pricing in different currencies?

Yes, we can bill in £GBP and $USD and pricing is reflective on market price. Let us know which you’d prefer when you sign up.

## Does 5app offer discounts to charities / non-profits?

Yes! We love working with non-profits, so we offer a 20% discount to any registered charity or non-profit organisation.

## Does 5app offer a free trial?

Yes! [Request a demo](https://5app.com/book-a-demo?hsLang=en) with our team and we'd be happy to set you up.

## Is there a minimum contract or subscription length?

Our subscription terms are as simple as our pricing. All contracts are subject to a 12-month minimum term, with some exceptions to shared Hub subscriptions.

## Is support included in the pricing?

Customer and technical support comes as part of our pricing, along with a dedicated CSM. The only additional service fee comes from technical integration work which needs bespoke planning and delivery.

## Will you help me migrate my existing content?

Yes! It's our job to make your job easier. Speak to our team and let us know what you need.

## Are there any hidden fees or additional charges?

Nope, nada, nothing. The price you see is what you pay. Simple pricing for a simple solution.

## Can I change my subscription plan later?

Yes, we make it easy for you to add or remove services. We know that constant change can cause headaches for your business, so we’re here to help you adapt, whatever happens.

## Will my data be private and safe?

Always! Your data protection and privacy is as much of a priority as ours. Our product is totally secure, and whatever data you share with us, stays with us.
