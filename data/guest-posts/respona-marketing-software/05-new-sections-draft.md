## What is marketing software?

Marketing software is any platform that helps a marketer or marketing team plan, execute, measure, or scale their marketing efforts. It is a broad category that covers email marketing, marketing automation, marketing analytics, content marketing, SEO, customer engagement, and customer support software. A typical modern marketing stack uses eight to twelve different marketing software categories at once, which is exactly the problem this article is trying to solve.

Marketing software differs from generic SaaS in one important way. Almost every tool in the category is built around a single marketing function (email, video, social, analytics) but markets itself as a complete marketing solution. The buyer's job is to figure out which marketing tool actually owns its category and which is stretching its definition.
