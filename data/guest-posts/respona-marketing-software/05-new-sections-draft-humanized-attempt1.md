## What is marketing software?

Marketing software is the term for any platform that makes it easier for individual marketers or marketing teams to plan, launch, optimize, or scale their marketing. This category encompasses:

- email marketing
- marketing automation
- marketing analytics
- content marketing
- SEO
- customer engagement
- customer support software

Modern marketers tend to use between 8-12 of these different software categories at a time, which makes the sheer size and complexity of this market one of the most significant problems this article is trying to solve.

However, marketing software is also different from standard SaaS in an important way. Nearly every piece of marketing software you can find is designed around a single marketing task (email marketing or video marketing) yet they all position themselves as a holistic marketing solution for businesses and teams to leverage. The challenge for marketers, then, becomes figuring out which software products have defined the category and which ones are expanding their definition to encompass multiple categories.
