Overview of Clipchamp

Whether you need a video for your work, for educational purposes, or because you want to create your own video content, Clipchamp is an excellent place to start, as a free version is available.

Clipchamp is also a software with a user-friendly interface that can be used online or downloaded if you have a PC.

Pros of Clipchamp

  • Top-notch filters and transitions.
  • High-resolution exports up to 1080p.
  • No video editing skills needed.
  • No watermarks for videos under 5 min.
  • Intuitive interface, suitable for beginners.
  • Soundtracks and animation material.
  • Editable video templates for different platforms.
  • You can easily share the video clips on diverse social media platforms.

Cons of Clipchamp

  • The free version is limited in storage and editing tools
  • Video resolutions higher than 1080p are only available to paid users.
  • Only PC owners can download the software.
  • Basic set of editing functions.

Pricing

The prices to subscribe to one of the ClimChamp plans will differ depending on whether you use the online version or download the PC software.

In the case of the online editor, Clipchamp has two plans:

  • Free: Get certain access to certain Clipchamp video editing tools.
  • Essentials: You can have different benefits for a few dollars per month.

Alternative #2: Movavi

Best for those content creators looking for attractive effects

Movavi is an easy-to-use video editing software with an incredible variety of effects, such as transitions, filters, and stickers. That's why it's a great alternative to making creative content that stands out.

As a bonus, the Movavi video editor is available for Windows and macOS, making it accessible to anyone with a computer.

Pros of Movavi

  • Friendly editing software.
  • Free audio files and visual elements.
  • Effects Store with more than 6000 extra content elements
  • Fast 4K video processing.
  • Multiple export alternatives.
  • Green screen.
  • Overlay, color correction.
  • AI-based audio denoiser.
  • Instant sharing to social media channels.

Cons of Movavi

  • The effects available in the store are usually expensive.
  • You cannot record sounds in the free version, and it will have a watermark.
  • This software has less advanced video editing features than other professional video editors.

Pricing

Movavi pricing packages are:

  • Video Editor: $49.95 per year.
  • Editor + effects packages: For only $69.95.
  • Video Suite + Effects Packs: Customers will pay $94.9 for this subscription.

How does it compare to Clipchamp?

Although Clipchamp is an editor that allows you to make videos for any platform, Movavi's many special effects and designs make it a better choice for content creators.

Also, unlike Clipchamp, Movavi uses artificial intelligence to refine certain video editing features.

Alternative #3: Vimeo

Best for video streaming

Next on our list, we have Vimeo.

Vimeo is an excellent Climchamp alternative that works both as a video editor and video host so that you can see Vimeo as a mix between a video clip editor and Youtube.

With Vimeo, you can create world-class videos, keep your content organized, and broadcast live events, making it perfect for content creators interested in live streaming.

Pros of Vimeo

  • Screen recording.
  • Easy to replace or re-upload a video.
  • Add free video hosting platform.
  • Customizable overlays and AI-powered hotspots.
  • You can monetize your content with an OTT.
  • Various integrations.
  • Stock video clips.

Cons of Vimeo

  • Storage limit.
  • Less visibility and reach for your videos as they are less popular than Youtube.
  • Basic editing tools.

Pricing

At Vimeo, there are four types of memberships billed annually.

  • Starter: for $12 per month.
  • Standard: for $20 per month.
  • Advance: for $65 monthly.
  • Enterprise: There is a custom alternative for Vimeo clients.

How does it compare to Clipchamp?

The most significant difference between these two platforms is that Vimeo, apart from being a video editor, is a video hosting similar to YouTube, which allows you to make live videos.

Another difference is that while Vimeo has basic tools for editing your video, they don't compare to what Clipchamp and other alternatives offer on this list.

Alternative #4: iMovie

Best for macOS and iOS users

iMovie is a video editing software developed by Apple, designed specifically for macOS and iOS users. It offers a simple and intuitive interface with several basic editing tools and effects.

Pros of iMovie

  • Free and pre-installed on macOS and iOS devices.
  • Intuitive interface.
  • Cinematic filters.
  • Video converter.
  • Easy to edit videos from different devices apple devices.
  • Audio editing.
  • Text, video, and graphic overlays.
  • Image stabilizer.
  • Integration with other Apple products is seamless.

Cons of iMovie

  • This software does not provide advanced editing capabilities compared to professional-level software.
  • Available exclusively for Apple devices.

Pricing

  • Free: iMovie is a free video editor for Mac and iOS users.

How does it compare to Clipchamp?

The biggest difference between these two editors comes from their interface and the systems they are compatible with. On the one hand, iMovie is compatible with iOS and macOS as it is an Apple product, while Clipchamp works better with PC as a Microsoft product.

Another big difference is that iMovie doesn't have an online editor version, and ClipChamp does.
