## All your questions answered

### Is HeyGen AI free or paid?

HeyGen AI offers both free and paid plans. The **free plan** allows you to create short videos (up to 1 minute) with limited features, including access to 120+ avatars and 300+ voices. However, this plan is quite basic and includes only 1 credit for video creation.

### Is HeyGen safe?

HeyGen offers content moderation to ensure the videos produced are safe and requires consent for custom avatars. However, they allow users to create avatars based on uploaded photos without the consent of the people in those photos, which could be used to generate harmful content.

### Is HeyGen worth it?

Overall, HeyGen is one of the better AI video generators on the market, but it falls short of the market leader, Synthesia. Synthesia starts at $18/month whereas HeyGens start at $24/month, so we recommend choosing Synthesia to take advantage of its superior features and more realistic avatars and voices.

### What is HeyGen?

HeyGen is a platform that offers AI-generated avatars for various applications, such as customer service and content creation. While HeyGen provides customizable digital avatars, it may not match the needs for collaboration and editing capabilities of some other platforms.

### How much is HeyGen?

Heygen offers several pricing plans:

- **Free**: $0 per month for 1 minute of video.
- **Creator**: $24 per month (max. 5 minutes per video).
- **Teams:** $69 per month (max. 60 minutes per video).
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing based on specific needs.

### Who owns HeyGen?

HeyGen was started in 2020 in China. It is a privately held company owned by Joshua Xu and Wayne Liang. Xu is the CEO of the company, and Liang is also a co-founder.

### What are HeyGen competitors?

HeyGen alternatives in the AI video creation space include:

1. **Synthesia**: Known for its advanced features, realistic avatars, and superior voice cloning capabilities, Synthesia is often regarded as a top choice for professional-grade AI video production.
2. **Colossyan**: Offers a similar platform for creating videos with AI-generated avatars, though it is generally seen as less robust than Synthesia in terms of customization and avatar realism.
3. **Rephrase**: Focuses on personalized video creation with AI avatars, offering multilingual support and easy-to-use tools, though it may lack some of the advanced features found in Synthesia.
4. **Pictory**: While more focused on converting text content into video, Pictory is a competitor in the AI video space, offering quick and easy video creation with minimal effort.
5. **DeepBrain AI**: Specializes in AI video synthesis with a focus on lifelike avatars and real-time processing, making it a strong competitor in the space.

Each of these platforms offers unique features, but Synthesia is often considered the most advanced in terms of quality and functionality.
