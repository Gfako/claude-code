FAQs

AI training video FAQs

Can AI make training videos?

Yes, AI can create professional training videos by converting text into engaging video content with AI avatars, voiceovers, and visual elements. Synthesia eliminates the need for traditional training video production resources like cameras, studios, or actors, making it possible to produce high-quality training materials in minutes. This is one of the most practical applications of AI for instructional design.

AI-powered video creation is particularly valuable for L&D teams who need to scale their employee training videos globally. You can create consistent training content in over 140 languages, update videos instantly when information changes, and maintain brand standards across all materials. This approach helps organizations reduce training costs while improving engagement and retention.

Whether you're producing compliance training videos, cyber security training videos, leadership training videos, or software training videos, AI makes it faster and more scalable than ever before.

Do I need cameras, actors, or voiceover talent to create training videos with Synthesia?

No, you don't need any traditional video production equipment or talent to create training videos with Synthesia. The platform uses AI avatars as presenters and generates natural-sounding voiceovers from your text, handling all the visual and audio elements automatically.

Instead of coordinating schedules with actors or booking studios, you simply type or paste your content into the platform. If you’re unsure where to start, you can use these free training video templates, free training video script templates, or learn how to write a training video script that keeps learners engaged.

This streamlined workflow dramatically lowers the cost of training employees while allowing teams to focus on strategy and instructional design rather than production logistics.

Can I translate and localize training videos into multiple languages with Synthesia?

Yes, Synthesia enables instant translation and localization of training videos into over 140 languages with just one click.

This capability is transformative for global organizations delivering interactive training videos or scenario-based training across multiple regions. The multilingual video player automatically detects viewers' language preferences, ensuring each employee receives training in their preferred language without manual intervention.

By combining localization with AI production, organizations can scale training globally while maintaining consistency, accuracy, and cultural relevance.

Can I turn existing PDFs, slides, or web pages into training videos?

Yes, Synthesia's AI Video Assistant can automatically convert training materials into engaging videos, including PDFs, PowerPoint presentations, and web pages.

Simply upload your document or paste a URL, and the AI analyzes the content to create a structured video script complete with visuals and narration. This feature is especially useful for teams looking to make training more engaging without starting from scratch.

Instead of rebuilding content manually, you can transform static documents into dynamic training experiences that improve comprehension, retention, and completion rates.

Is Synthesia secure and compliant for enterprise training content (SOC 2, GDPR)?

Yes, Synthesia is SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant, meeting the strict security requirements needed for enterprise training content. The platform implements comprehensive security measures to protect sensitive training materials and employee data throughout the video creation and distribution process.

Security is built into every aspect of the platform, from encrypted data storage to controlled access permissions for team collaboration. This commitment to data protection means L&D teams can confidently create training videos containing proprietary information, compliance materials, or sensitive company procedures. The platform's enterprise-grade security infrastructure ensures your training content remains protected while still being easily accessible to authorized learners across your organization.
