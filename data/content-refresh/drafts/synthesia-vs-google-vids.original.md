## AI video generation FAQs

### **What is Google Vids?**

Google Vids is an AI-powered video creation tool that’s part of the Google Workspace suite. It helps users turn scripts into narrated videos using slides, stock visuals, and AI-generated voiceovers. While it’s designed for simplicity and collaboration, especially for users already using Google Docs or Slides, it’s still early in development compared to more advanced platforms like Synthesia. Google Vids currently lacks features like real-time interactivity, multilingual support, or enterprise-level customization. Avatars are expected to roll out to Labs users soon.

### **What are the best Google Vids alternatives?**

The top alternatives to Google Vids include Synthesia, Colossyan, and Pictory:

- **Synthesia** is often the preferred choice for businesses looking to scale content production across training, communications, and localization. It offers lifelike AI avatars, advanced voice cloning, support for over 140 languages, and deep enterprise capabilities including SCORM export, branding kits, analytics, and 1-click translation. It’s a strong fit for mid-market and enterprise teams who need more than just simple video narration.
- **Colossyan** is another AI video tool that supports avatars and multilingual output. While it enables quick creation of simple videos with avatars, it lacks advanced editing tools, real-time collaboration, AI dubbing, and other features needed for scalable, brand-consistent content creation in enterprise environments.
