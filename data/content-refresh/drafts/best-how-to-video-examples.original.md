## Who can benefit from a how-to video?

Anyone from independent content creators to companies selling physical/digital products benefits from creating this type of content that nearly [30% of internet users](https://www.oberlo.com/statistics/online-video-consumption-statistics) want to see.

A how-to video could be anything from a marketing video to a video tutorial. Some companies even use them to create explainer videos, a great tool for advertising!

How-to videos are helpful for both external and internal uses. With them, you can:

✅ Teach others about products, services, or concepts

✅ Introduce employees to internal processes

✅ Guide customers through troubleshooting steps

✅ Make tedious procedures easier to learn

✅ And much more!
