## 🚨 Core Pain Points

- **"What actually counts as microlearning vs just a short video?"** — PAA literally surfaces *"Is microlearning a real thing?"* and *"What are microlearning videos?"*. Buyers still need a fast, defensible definition with a duration range.
- **"I can write one good 2-minute video. I can't produce 50 of them across 10 languages."** — every ranking page (Docebo, eLI, PPAI, NinjaTropic) treats production as a single-video problem. Real L&D teams need a system.
- **"My microlearning library is already going stale."** — when product, policy, or tools change, every short video is broken until re-shot. Nobody on the SERP addresses this maintenance/refresh cost.
- **"How do I prove this works?"** — competitors say "track completion rates and quiz scores" and stop there. Buyers need a defensible measurement framework before they get budget.
- **"Which format do I actually pick?"** — the SERP has scattered format lists (kinetic typography, whiteboard, demo, scenario, etc.) but no clean decision rule for *which* format fits *which* use case.
