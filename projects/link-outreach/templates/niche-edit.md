# Niche Edit Email Template

## Context for Claude
When drafting niche edit outreach emails, follow these rules:
- Reference a SPECIFIC section or sentence from their article — prove you read it
- Explain WHY linking to the Synthesia page adds value for their readers (not for us)
- Keep it under 80 words
- No HTML, no images, no links in the first email except the suggested URL
- Sound like a human, not a template
- Soft CTA — ask if they'd be open to it, don't demand

## Email Structure

**Subject line:** 4-5 words max, reference their article topic. Examples:
- "Quick suggestion for your {topic} article"
- "Resource for your {topic} piece"
- "Thought for your {topic} guide"

**Body:**
```
Hi {first_name},

I was reading your piece on {article_topic} — specifically where you {reference_specific_section}.

We have a {synthesia_page_description} that {specific_value_add}. I think it'd be a useful addition there for readers looking to {reader_benefit}.

Would you be open to adding it?

Cheers,
{sender_name}
```

## Follow-up 1 (3 days later)
```
Hi {first_name},

Just bumping this up — did you get a chance to look at {synthesia_url}? Happy to explain how it fits into your {article_topic} section if helpful.

{sender_name}
```

## Follow-up 2 (5 days later)
```
Hi {first_name},

Last follow-up on this — totally understand if it's not a fit. Just thought it could genuinely help your readers in that section about {specific_section}.

Either way, great article.

{sender_name}
```
