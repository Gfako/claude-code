# Guest Post Email Template

## Context for Claude
When drafting guest post pitches:
- Reference a SPECIFIC recent article on their blog to show you know the site
- Pitch 2 topic ideas that genuinely fit their audience
- Each topic should naturally allow linking to a Synthesia page
- Keep under 100 words
- Don't say "guest post" in the subject line (overused, triggers spam)
- Position yourself as an expert contributor, not a link builder

## Email Structure

**Subject line:** Examples:
- "Content idea for {blog_name}"
- "Article pitch: {topic_idea}"
- "Contribution idea for your blog"

**Body:**
```
Hi {first_name},

I've been reading {blog_name} — your recent piece on {recent_article_topic} was particularly good.

I'd love to contribute an article on one of these topics:

1. {topic_pitch_1} — {one_sentence_summary}
2. {topic_pitch_2} — {one_sentence_summary}

Both would be original, practical, and backed by real examples. I can have a draft ready within a week.

Would either of these work for your editorial calendar?

{sender_name}
```

## Follow-up 1 (4 days later)
```
Hi {first_name},

Wanted to check if either of those topic ideas could work for {blog_name}. Happy to adjust the angle or suggest alternatives if needed.

{sender_name}
```

## Follow-up 2 (6 days later)
```
Hi {first_name},

Last ping on this — I know editorial calendars fill up fast. If the timing isn't right now, I'm happy to revisit in a few weeks.

{sender_name}
```
